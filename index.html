The Penthouses

A duo of exquisite penthouses where architecture, art, design, and landscape blend in a seamless expression of elevated island living.

Two names, two penthouses, one singular vision. Nestled on the island's West coast, these design-led residences with sweeping views of the Indian Ocean are an intimate expression of art, design, and sense of place—where curated interiors meet the natural and dramatic beauty of the island of Mauritius. Crafted with soul and precision, every detail tells a story: of family, of nature, of beauty in motion. More than homes, JOA and LOE are living, breathing works of art—serene by day, spellbinding by night.

JOA

One Island, One Address

Mauritius, a jewel in the Indian Ocean, is famed for its natural beauty, refined island living, and year-round tropical warmth. The sun-drenched west coast strikes a unique balance between wild, untamed nature and a vibrant, connected community. It’s home to a growing circle of like-minded people—a blend of in-the-know locals and expats who have fallen for the rhythm of the West. Life here moves with ease. Top-tier schools, quality healthcare, cafés, restaurants, and local businesses sit alongside splendid nature: surf breaks in Tamarin, Le Morne lagoon, the trails of Black River Gorges National Park, Chamarel… It’s the perfect blend of island authenticity and modern convenience. Here, residents enjoy quiet luxury, outdoor living, and a strong sense of place and belonging.
