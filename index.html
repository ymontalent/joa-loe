JOA & LOE

A duo of exquisite penthouses where architecture, art, design, and landscape blend in a seamless expression of elevated island living.

Two names, two penthouses, one singular vision. Nestled on the island's West coast, these design-led residences with sweeping views of the Indian Ocean are an intimate expression of art, design, and sense of place—where curated interiors meet the natural and dramatic beauty of the island of Mauritius. Crafted with soul and precision, every detail tells a story: of family, of nature, of beauty in motion. More than homes, JOA and LOE are living, breathing works of art—serene by day, spellbinding by night.

SEAMLESS ACCESS TO A
HILLSIDE BOUTIQUE RESORT

While JOA and LOE offer the privacy of standalone residences, they come with the rare advantage of seamless access to a nearby boutique resort: Legend Hill. Just steps away, guests can enjoy a sundowner at the stylish poolside bar, check into the spa, or indulge in cuisine crafted by Michelin-starred chef Glenn Viel, at the hotel’s signature restaurant. It’s the best of both worlds: the intimacy of a private home paired with the perks of a boutique resort.

THE BEST SPOT

Perched high above the glittering West Coast in Tamarin, on La Tourelle mountain, the penthouses occupy a rare position—both literally and metaphorically. Carved into the cliffside like natural sculpture, they command panoramic views of Le Morne’s mythic silhouette and the infinite blues of the Indian Ocean. Sunsets are cinematic. From this unique vantage, guests feel cradled by nature yet connected to it. JOA and LOE don’t just sit at the top of the mountain; they sit at the intersection of sky, sea, and story. This is not just a good view. This is the view.

CURATED ISLAND-LIVING EXPERIENCES

JOA and LOE connect guests to the island's culture, landscape, and rhythm in a meaningful way. Your butler can plan island experiences and exclusive outings, but also bring the spirit of the island indoors: think a rum-tasting on the deck at sunset, a celebratory dinner crafted by the Chef using local, seasonal produce, a family cooking class, a serene massage on your terrace. The essence of Mauritius is infused into each and every curated experience.

space & layout

Each penthouse has three beautifully appointed suites, each with their unique design and individual bathrooms. Two of the bedrooms offer mesmerising seaviews. A fourth multipurpose room with a bespoke desk offers a space to reflect. The space may be converted into a bedroom for the Nanny or another member of the personnel. The infinity pool with its exotic wood deck is adjacent to the tisanerie with sitting chairs, connecting the interiors to the outside. The gorgeous kitchen with its marble countertop and carved wood-pannelled walls, is linked to a secondary discrete kitchen for the personnel Chef.

meet the architect

Olivier Gossart founded his namesake agency,O.GOSSART, in Paris nearly two decades ago. A keen observer of new ways of living, he belongs to a generation of creative thinkers drawn to complexity—constantly exploring new ways to shape both collective and intimate spaces. The studio works across hospitality, residential, workplace, and yachting projects, crafting interiors that are at once sustainable, concept-driven, and deeply personal. Each project is approached as a distinct universe, where architecture, scenography, and design converge in a unified, thoughtful narrative. Guided by continuous curiosity, a taste for challenge, and a refined sense of aesthetics, Gossart’s practice reflects a vision for a new art of living—one attuned to a society in flux. To bring this vision to life, the agency collaborates with a diverse circle of artists, lighting designers, acousticians, landscape architects, and creators from the worlds of fashion and fragrance.
